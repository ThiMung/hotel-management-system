NMT Hotel History

Built in 1898, the red-brick building that now houses NMT Hotel began its life as a riverside warehouse along the East River, where sailors unloaded crates of coffee, spices, and textiles from ships bound for the growing city...

In 2011, a young architect stumbled upon the forgotten structure ... Over two years of careful restoration, every window was mended by hand, every steel beam polished but not hidden.
