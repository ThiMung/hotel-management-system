SINGLE ROOMS

Our Single Room is perfect for one person. It is clean, cozy, and has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The room includes a soft bed, air conditioning, a TV, a desk, and a private bathroom. You can see the city from the window. The hotel is in the city center, close to shops, restaurants, and tourist places. It is a great choice for people traveling alone for work or vacation.

DOUBLE ROOMS

Our Double Room is perfect for two people or couples. The room is large, clean, and comfortable. It has a big soft bed, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a desk, a wardrobe, and a private bathroom. From the window, you can see the city or the garden view. The hotel is near the city center, so you can easily go to restaurants, shops, and tourist places. The Double Room gives you a quiet and romantic space to relax and enjoy your stay.

FAMILY ROOMS

Our Family Room is great for families or small groups. It is big, bright, and very comfortable. The room has one large bed and one or two small beds, air conditioning, a TV, a table, and a private bathroom. There is also free Wi-Fi and a small sitting area. From the window, you can see the city or the garden. The hotel is close to restaurants, shops, and fun places for children. The Family Room is a good choice for relaxing and enjoying time together.

VIP ROOMS

Our VIP Room is the best choice for guests who want luxury and comfort. The room is very large and beautiful, with a big soft bed, air conditioning, a large TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom with a bathtub. There is also a nice sofa and a city view from the window. Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi, room service, and special welcome drinks. The VIP Room gives you a peaceful, relaxing, and high-class stay experience.
